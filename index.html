Guiyun Fan (范桂云)

I'm currently an associate professor in School of Computer Science and Technology, TongJi University. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the John Hopcroft Center (JHC) for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I received my PhD degree in information and communication engineering with Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2021. I received my Bachelor degree from Xidian Universityin July 2016.

My research interest lies in intelligent internet of things, urban computing, smart transportation, reinforcement learning in networks and systems, and data-driven researches in the intersection of AI, machine learning, and networks and systems. Recently, I have developed a profound interest in compute-network integration and computing-aware routing, cloud and edge computing, and network economics.

For any student who is interested in aforementioned (but not limited to those) areas, please feel free to shoot me an email. Both undergraduate and graduate students, are welcomed!
