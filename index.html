Guiyun Fan (范桂云)

I'm currently an associate professor in School of Computer Science and Technology, TongJi University. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the John Hopcroft Center (JHC) for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I received my PhD degree in information and communication engineering with Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2021. I received my Bachelor degree from Xidian University in July 2016.

My research interest lies in addressing various challenges in applying reinforcement learning algorithms to practical sequential decision-making applications. These challenges include, but are not limited to, satisfying constraint conditions, enhancing robustness, and facilitating multi-agent interaction. Recently, I have developed a profound interest in interpretable policies, particularly the programmatic policy class.
